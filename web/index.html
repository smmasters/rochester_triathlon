Rochester Triathlon

Welcome to the 10th Annual Rochester Triathlon

The tenth annual Rochester (NY) Triathlon takes place on Sunday August 22, 2021 at Durand Beach, starting at 8:00am. Both the Sprint and Intermediate distance bike courses feature scenic park views and will have the majority of their routes closed to traffic. This takes place along side the Rochester Duathlon.

A huge thank you to the Rochester Area Triathletes who host this event. Become a member and train with them.

We greatly appreciate our volunteers and would love to have you as part of our team.

Event Details
Saturday, August 21: Pre-Race Day Events (Tentative)
4:00pm - 7:00pm - Packet Pickup at Towpath Bike (map)
- Stock up on tubes, apparel and all your nutrition needs!
- You must show up in person with your ID; including each of the 3 relay members

Sunday, August 22: Race Day Schedule (Tentative)
6:00am - 7:15am - Packet Pickup tent opens for registered athletes who still need race packets (sorry, no race day registration)
- Transition area opens (your bike must have the bike number affixed to enter transition)
- Body marking will take place in front of transition area
7:30am Transition area closes
7:40am Pre-race meeting at swim start, on the beach for ALL athletes
8:00am The first Olympic wave starts, followed by the rest of the Olympic waves in 4 minute intervals
8:30am The first Sprint waves starts, followed by the rest of the Sprint waves in 4 minute intervals
10:00am - 11:30am Post race food and festivities will take place near the finish line
11:30am Awards
